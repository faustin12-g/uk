About Umuturage Kw’isonga (UK)

Bringing Citizens Closer to Public Service

Our Mission

At Umuturage Kw’isonga (UK), our mission is to improve citizen satisfaction by providing a reliable, user-friendly platform for raising complaints and feedback about public services. We aim to make sure that no voice goes unheard and that all citizens can easily interact with the institutions that serve them.

For Citizens

Citizens can register on the platform and submit complaints or suggestions related to public services. Each submission is categorized and routed to the responsible institution.

  • Submit complaints or feedback easily from web or mobile.
  • Track the status of your submissions in real-time.

For Government Agencies

Institutions receive categorized submissions directly and can respond through a simple admin dashboard, ensuring timely action and improved accountability.

  • Quickly access and manage incoming complaints.
  • Respond to citizens and update ticket status.

Transparency
